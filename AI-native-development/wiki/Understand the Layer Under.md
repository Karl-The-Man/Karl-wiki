# Understand the Layer Under

[[Boris Cherny]]'s long-standing engineering advice: always understand the abstraction layer below the one you work at.

## The Principle

Previously this meant: if you write application code, understand the VM or runtime. If you work on the runtime, understand the OS. Each layer of understanding makes you more effective at your own layer.

## Applied to AI-Native Development

Now, the "layer under" for AI-native developers is **the model itself**. Boris spent time studying reinforcement learning at [[Anthropic]] specifically to understand the layer beneath [[Claude Code]].

Understanding how the model works — its strengths, failure modes, how it responds to different prompting strategies — is the new version of understanding your runtime.
