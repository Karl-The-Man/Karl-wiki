# Jack Dorsey

Co-founder and head of [[Block]] (formerly Square). Previously co-founded Twitter. Author of [[From Hierarchy to Intelligence]], a manifesto arguing that AI can replace organizational hierarchy as an information routing mechanism.

## Role in AI-Native Development

Dorsey is significant not as a technologist building AI tools, but as a **CEO redesigning an entire company around AI**. While [[Boris Cherny]] and [[Andrej Karpathy]] describe AI-native *individual workflows*, Dorsey is attempting AI-native *organizational design* — replacing the management hierarchy with a system of [[World Model (Organizational)|world models]] and an [[Intelligence Layer]].

His framing — from Sequoia's vantage point — positions speed as the ultimate competitive advantage, and hierarchy as the primary impediment to speed. This extends the wiki's AI-native thesis from "how individuals work" to "how companies are structured."

## Key Ideas

- [[Hierarchy as Information Routing]] — hierarchy exists because of human span-of-control limits, not because it's the best organizational form
- [[Company as Intelligence]] — replace what hierarchy does with AI that maintains a continuously updated model of the business
- Three roles (ICs, DRIs, player-coaches) replace the traditional management pyramid
- "If AI is just cost optimization, you get absorbed" — the real play is compounding understanding, not cutting headcount
