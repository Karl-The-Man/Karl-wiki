# Company as Intelligence

The thesis that a company should be organized as a unified intelligence system rather than a human hierarchy. Articulated by [[Jack Dorsey]] in [[From Hierarchy to Intelligence]] as [[Block]]'s strategic direction.

## Core Idea

> "In a conventional company, the intelligence is spread throughout the people and the hierarchy routes it. In this model, the intelligence lives in the system. The people are on the edge."

Traditional companies distribute intelligence across employees and use hierarchy to route it. The "company as intelligence" model inverts this: intelligence lives in the system (AI-maintained [[World Model (Organizational)|world models]] + [[Intelligence Layer]]), and people operate at the edge — reaching into places the model can't go, sensing what it can't perceive, making calls it shouldn't make.

## How It Differs from "AI as Copilot"

> "Most companies using AI today are giving everyone a copilot, which makes the existing structure work slightly better without changing it."

The copilot model preserves the existing hierarchy and makes individual workers more productive. The "company as intelligence" model **replaces what the hierarchy does** — information routing, alignment, priority-setting, coordination — with AI systems. This is a structural transformation, not a productivity enhancement.

| | AI as Copilot | Company as Intelligence |
|---|---|---|
| **Hierarchy** | Preserved | Replaced |
| **AI role** | Augments individuals | Coordinates the organization |
| **Roadmap** | PMs hypothesize | Customer reality generates backlog |
| **Middle management** | Still needed | Eliminated |
| **Information flow** | Same channels, faster | Fundamentally re-routed |
| **Outcome** | Cost optimization | Compounding understanding |

## Architecture

Four layers power the intelligence:

1. **Capabilities** — atomic primitives (in Block's case: payments, lending, banking, etc.)
2. **[[World Model (Organizational)|World Model]]** — company operations + customer reality
3. **[[Intelligence Layer]]** — composes capabilities into proactive solutions
4. **Interfaces** — delivery surfaces

## The "Get Absorbed" Warning

> "If the answer is nothing, AI is just a cost optimization story. You cut headcount, improve margins for a few quarters, and eventually get absorbed by something smarter."

Companies that use AI only for productivity get a temporary margin improvement. Companies that build compounding understanding — a world model that gets richer every day — create a durable competitive advantage.

## Relationship to AI-Native Workflow

This concept extends the [[AI-Native Workflow]] from the individual to the organizational level:

- **Individual AI-native workflow**: human orchestrates agents to write code ([[Boris Cherny]], [[Andrej Karpathy]])
- **Organizational AI-native design**: AI orchestrates the company, humans operate at the edge ([[Jack Dorsey]], [[Block]])

Both share the same structural move: AI takes over the coordination function that humans used to perform.

## Open Questions

- Can this work outside fintech, where transaction data provides uniquely rich signal?
- How does accountability work without a management chain?
- What happens when the world model is wrong at scale?
- How do career progression and mentorship work with only three roles?
- Block is "in the early stages" — will the vision survive contact with reality?
