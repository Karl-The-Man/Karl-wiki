# Letta

Company focused on stateful agents, described by [[Harrison Chase]] as having "consistently been at the forefront of stateful agents." CTO is [[Sarah Wooders]].

## Significance

Letta's work centers on the problem that most agent frameworks treat as an afterthought: [[Agent Memory]] and persistent state. Their Letta Code product is cited as one of the emerging [[Agent Harness|agent harnesses]] alongside [[Claude Code]], [[Deep Agents]], [[Codex]], and [[OpenClaw]].
