# Caltech

California Institute of Technology. Private research university in Pasadena, California. Home institution of [[Richard Feynman]] from 1950 until his death in 1988. Site of the 1974 commencement address in which Feynman delivered [[Cargo Cult Science (Richard Feynman)|"Cargo Cult Science"]], introducing the concepts of [[Scientific Integrity]] and [[Cargo Cult Science]] to a graduating class.
