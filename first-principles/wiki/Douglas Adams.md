# Douglas Adams

Author of *The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy*. [[Elon Musk]] interprets Adams's work as saying "The universe is the answer" — the challenge is figuring out what questions to ask, not finding answers. This reading directly informs Musk's [[Philosophy of Curiosity]]: expand consciousness to better frame the questions. *(elon-musk-on-first-principles.pdf)*
